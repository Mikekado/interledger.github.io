Get Involved

Want to build on Interledger or be part of its development?
Here's where you can find us.

Interledger Workshop - London, July 6

Join us for the 2nd Interledger Community Workshop in London (or online). We'll be discussing the specs and showing off updates to the reference implementation and demos. Come with questions, ideas and projects you want to Interledger-enable.

W3C Mailing List

All announcements, including about upcoming in-person meetings, and many technical discussions are done through the W3C Interledger Community Group Mailing List. Have ideas to share or just want to keep up with the latest developments? Join the Community Group.

IRC

Have a question or want to chat about Interledger? Join us on IRC: #interledger on irc.w3.org or use the web client.

Twitter

Follow @interledger on Twitter for the latest news and annoucements.

Github

All of the specs and component reference implementations are being developed on Github. Pull requests welcome!
